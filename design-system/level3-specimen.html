Level 3 Integration — Component & Content Specimen

Three handoff patterns for how a WRMP exhibit points a viewer to deeper resources — inline per-step, end-card hub, persistent drawer. Rounds 1 and 2 explored fifteen options and their refinements; round 3 is convergence — the locked, polished final of the one option the client chose per pattern. Rounds 1 and 2 stay below for history; the Round 3 · Final block in each pattern is the implementation target.

▾

The locked system: Data · Tools · Science is the organizing taxonomy across all three patterns, each category carrying one fixed Lucide icon and accent everywhere (Data · database · teal, Tools · compass · green, Science · flask · amber). The round-3 cross-cutting requirement: every Level 3 resource carries a real one-line description of what the viewer will find. The dark "deeper-data" identity from round 1 holds throughout.

Pattern 1 · Inline per-step

A dark "go deeper" module at the foot of a story-panel step, linking to the one Level 3 resource that matches that step. Sits inside a light panel — so it's a self-contained dark mini-module. The ladder adds content substance rung by rung.
