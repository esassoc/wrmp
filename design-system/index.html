Content hierarchy inside a step: kicker → h2 → body. The kicker provides thematic context; h2 is the narrative beat title.

Biodiversity

Native vs. Invasive

Of the 237 species recorded, 113 are native fish and 38 are native invertebrates. A dramatic regime shift occurred during the 2014–2015 drought.

Invasive species now dominate biomass in South Bay and Suisun regions during dry years.
