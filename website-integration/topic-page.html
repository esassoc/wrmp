Baylands Geography

Location and size of marshes, tidal flats, transition zones, riparian corridors, and channel networks

The WRMP is mapping changes over time in marsh and mudflat extents, bayland elevation capital, shoreline erosion and progradation, and the extent of unvegetated areas (pannes, ponds, and channels). Measurements from reference marshes are also measuring elevation change and sediment deposition to evaluate accretion rates.
